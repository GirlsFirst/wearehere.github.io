Addiction

Everyone eats or drinks too much sometimes, but addiction runs much deeper than this. Addicition is scary and often comes with other illnesses, and it's not a choice. Here you can find resources to aid and continue your recovery, or begin the journey.

Know you are not alone and you can fight this. Recovery and rehabilitation are always possible. If you are in immediate danger, call 911 or a crisis line.
